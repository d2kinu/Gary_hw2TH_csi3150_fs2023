Red Rising is not like any other dystopian fiction book. Thes story follows Darrow(Red), through his journey of becoming a Gold and setting his people free. This doesn't come without sacrifice. He must fake his death and leave everything he knows and become something he hates. Darrow sacrifices everything to infiltrate the legendary Institute, a proving ground for the dominant Gold caste, where the next generation of humanity’s overlords struggle for power. He was forced to compete for his life and the very future of civilization against the best and most brutal of Society’s ruling class.
